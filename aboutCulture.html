Our culture

Our focus is on people.

At KCPE, our team values meaningful relationships. That's why the people who join KCPE stay here.

A global leader in real estate.

Our team fosters genuine camaraderie and close collaboration, free from the sense of distance often found in the workplace, and nurtures a warm atmosphere of mutual support, shared achievements, and collective growth. It is this strong sense of belonging that inspires every member joining KCPE to commit to long-term engagement and shared progress.

Shared values for shared success.

The diversity of perspectives, experiences, and ideas is our core driving force to navigate market cycles and achieve sustainable success. We actively listen to diverse voices, enhance participation across multiple groups, and propel the industry toward greater diversity and inclusivity

integrity

We adopt a principled and diligent approach across allbusiness areas, ensuring every decision adheres to the highest ethical standards.

transparency

Our culture thrives on collaboration. We prioritize transparent and timely communication with all stakeholders to build mutual trust.

discipline

Real estate is our sole focus. We approach every investment with patience and discipline, reject ingreckless expansion.

reliability

We safeguard the longevity of our relationships and the trusted reputation built over decades, honoring every commitment.

analysis

We are data-driven, rejecting subjective assumptions and allowing objective evidence to guide every decision.

people

We put our employees and industry relationships first, striving to build long-term, genuine partnerships

ESG + DEI

Your interests are our interests.

KCPE takes a purpose-driven approach to Environmental, Social & Governance Policy. Our commitment informs the decisions we make internally and challenges our employees to seek to have a meaningful effect in our communities.

  • We endeavor to consider the environmental impact from upgrading, developing or operating a property, and any identified material ESG factors are reviewed by the Investment Committee.

  • KCPE supports nonprofits aimed at improving the local and global community through donations, sponsorship, volunteering, and board participation.

     

  • Through our purpose-driven committees, we monitor and approve the firm’s investment and management decisions using in-place policies and procedures.

     

Professional Development

We build careers.

At KCPE, we foster a culture of continual personal and professional development. That’s how we’ve managed to attract, reward, and retain leading industry talent.

 

Top tier training

The company focuses on the long-term personal and professional growth of its employees, providing diversified development support such as mentorship programs, industry networking, and specialized training.

 

Empowering women leaders

It also prioritizes enhancing female leadership capabilities through dedicated empowerment activities and targeted guidance, aiming to attract, motivate, and retain top-tier industry talent.

 

Analyst training program

Learn from the best. Become the best.

Each year, KCPE welcomes a small class of students for our 10-week internship program that delivers the tools necessary to excel as an analyst in this industry.

Become an intern

4 weeks of intensive, tailored training

Interns receive top-tier training for success as a full-time analyst, including real estate fundamentals, Excel modeling (via Training the Street), and Walton Street’s proprietary models.

Small, ambitious cohort

We keep our class of interns small, typically just 5–7 students, which allows us to provide close mentorship and support each individual’s growth.

 

Hands-on opportunities

Interns apply their training by presenting their work to the broader team, demonstrating their strengths and aptitude through case studies, and contributing to active KCPE projects.

 

Mentorship and retention

Our mentorship program fosters professional development and inclusion. For the last three of four years, all of our full-time analyst hires were members of the previous intern class.
