Investment strategy

We have originated, acquired, and managed debt investments through multiple cycles.

We focus on originating and investing in performing Malaysian and Chinese market-based, private commercial real estate debt across the risk spectrum.

 

We believe our cross-platform sourcing network and relationships provide competitive advantages over traditional debt-only fund managers, including increased deal flow, access to real time market and performance information, enhanced underwriting rigor, and strong asset management experience.

The KCPE Debt Investment seeks to originate real estate debt investments with disciplined underwriting and loan structures that provide downside protection.

Strong alignment of interest with borrowers achieved through equity subordination and loan structure, extensive asset underwriting, due diligence, and third-party consultant review

 

Institutional borrowers with demonstrated track records investing in targeted asset classes and markets. Strong relationships with borrowers, brokers, and senior lenders to drive best in class debt executions

Performing loans with an emphasis on cash flowing assets to designed to deliver a current return and regular, periodic distributions

 

Prudent and conservative liability and leverage management

Value-add, Core-plus & Traditional Bridge Lending

Premium Bridge Loans.

Focusing on premium commercial real estate, we provide floating-rate bulk and mezzanine loans secured by transitional assets. We target investments with long- term growth potential and strong exit liquidity.

Stable Asset Loans.

We offer floating and fixed-rate full loans secured by Class A or near-stable commercial real estate. Focusing on premium markets, we serve our clients with stable cash flows. Through moderate leverage and prudent risk management, we deliver robust returns and downside protection.
